Privacy Policy

Ratio Coffee · Crows Nest, Sydney · Last updated 11 July 2026

Who we are

Ratio Coffee is a specialty coffee roaster based in Crows Nest, Sydney, Australia. This policy covers our website, online shop, and social media pages.

What we collect

When you place an order we collect your name, email address, and delivery details so we can roast, pack, and ship your coffee and send you an order confirmation. Payments are processed by Square; we never see or store your card details.

How we use it

Your information is used only to fulfil your orders, manage subscriptions you sign up for, and reply when you contact us. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of service providers to run the business: Square (payments and checkout), Supabase (secure data hosting), and Meta platforms (our Instagram and Facebook pages). Each handles data under its own privacy policy.

Data retention and your rights

We keep order records only as long as needed for accounting and legal obligations. You can ask us at any time to see, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
